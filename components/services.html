Transition to Adulthood

Turning 18 marks the onset of adult responsibility. For many young adults, this onset is delayed until college graduation and sometimes long after. Every new adult faces challenges in setting the foundation for their independent life. Issues with relationships, emancipation, and financial independence can create anxiety and tension that can be resolved with the support of a therapist.

Sarah will reason through life goals and transitions to help establish strategies for a strong adult foundation.
